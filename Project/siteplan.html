Caster's Codex site plan

Site purpose

This site provides basic information about creatures in a dnd setting both homebrew and cannon.

Scenario

My players want to do something not explained in any of the dnd guide books I have or I simply cant find it. How do I do x. "(in the scope of This assignment I will be covering what players might find when they kill a creature and what they might be able to do with unique items such as alchemical supplies or exotic materials.)"

Scenario

I would like to know more about a specific creature within this world, what information do you have that is not provided in the DMG, Monster Manual or Players Handbook

Light Mode

text will be black, header will have a background of blue and main will have a background of white. borders will be black.

Dark Mode

Text will be white with the exception of headers and titles which will be yellow along side accents, background will be a dark purple.
